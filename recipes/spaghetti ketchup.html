Spaghetti Ketchup

Description

Are you mad at the Italians for joining the Axis Powers? Are you disappointed that they don't actually eat shrooms and go jumping on each other? Then here's how you make them Mamma their last Mia!

Ingredients

  • Packaged Spaghetti
  • Heinz Ketchiup

Steps

  1. Cook the packaged Spaghetti according to the instructions on the box
  2. Drain the water and pasta using a collander
  3. Plate the spaghetti onto a plate
  4. Drench them noodles with ketchup until they are soaked red
  5. Present the dish to an Italian and get arrested

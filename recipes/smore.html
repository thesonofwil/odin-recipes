S'mores

Description

Your insurance company will thank you when you start chowing down on these delicacies!

Ingredients

  • Square Graham crackers
  • Marshmallow
  • Chocolate

Steps

  1. Prep a campfire
  2. Cut or snap your chocolate such that it fits onto one of the Graham crackers. The other Graham cracker will be used as the top side. Set aside
  3. Skewer a marshmallow onto a stick and roast it in the campfire, occassionally rotating until the outside of the marshmallow is a golden-brown throughout
  4. Take the marshmallow off and place it on top of the empty Graham cracker
  5. Stack the two Graham crackers on top of each other with the chocolate and marshmallow facing each other. The chocolate should begin to melt
  6. Wake up the next day to find yourself drowning with insulin bills

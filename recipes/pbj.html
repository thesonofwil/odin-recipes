Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Description

The old. The reliable. The classic. Who doesn't love a good-ole fashioned PBJ sandwich? Europeans apparently

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Slice the bread with a knife twice to get two slices
  2. (Optional) toast the slices of bread by putting them in a toaster
  3. Spread peanut butter using a knife onto one side of a slice fully
  4. Spread jelly using a knife onto one side of the other slice fully
  5. Stack the two slices on top of each other, spreads facing inwards towards each other, to form a sandwich
